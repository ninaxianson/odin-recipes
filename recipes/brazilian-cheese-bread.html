Pão de QueijoMakes 30 cheese rolls

Description

These hoagie rolls whip up quick and are easy to make. They are great for French-dip sandwiches, meatball bombers, and more!

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups tapioca flour
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 6 tbsp oil
  • 1 1/2 cups grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Place rack in middle position and preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Combine milk, water, oil, and salt in a saucepan and bring it to a boil over medium high heat.
  3. Add the tapioca flour to the bowl of a stand mixer and once the milk mixture boils, pour it over the flour. Turn mixer on and mix it well. The mixture will be fondant like, really white and sticky.
  4. With the mixer still on, add the eggs, one at a time. You will think they won't mix, but hang in there because it will.
  5. Once the eggs are incorporated, add the cheese, a little at a time, until fully incorporated. The dough is supposed to be soft and sticky. However, if it's too liquidy, add a little more tapioca flour. Just don't over do it.
  6. To shape the balls, wet your hands with cold water and, using a spoon, scoop some of the dough to shape balls that are a little smaller than golf ball sized.
  7. Place on baking tray, covered with parchment.
  8. Bake 15 to 20 minutes, or until they are golden and puffed. Serve warm.
