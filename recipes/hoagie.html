Hoagie Rolls8 servings

Description

These hoagie rolls whip up quick and are easy to make. They are great for French-dip sandwiches, meatball bombers, and more!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 warm water
  • 1 tsbp instant yeast
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 3 3/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 tbsp milk

Steps

  1. In the bowl of a stand mixer, add the warm water, yeast, sugar, and honey. Allow to foam 5 to 10 minutes.
  2. Add the flour and using dough hook mix for 4 minutes on low speed.
  3. Increase the speed to medium and add the salt, mixing for another 3 minutes.
  4. Finally, add the cold butter, one tablespoon at a time and mix for another minute or two after each addition. Continue to mix until no pieces of butter remain and the dough is silky and smooth.
  5. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, turning to coat, cover with a towel and let rise for 1 to 2 hours.
  6. Cover a baking tray with parchment paper. Shape dough into 8 hoagies. Divide dough into 8 equal pieces. Press each into a rectangle. Fold one half over like an envelope, then rotate the dough 180 degrees and fold the other end over the first. Pinch the seams together.
  7. Place on baking tray, cover lightly with a towel and let rise another 1 to 2 hours.
  8. Whisk together the egg white and milk. Using a sharp knife, make a slit down the top of each roll. Brush each top with the egg mixture.
  9. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees. Bake 16 to 22 minutes, or until they are lightly golden and feel hard when you tap the crust
